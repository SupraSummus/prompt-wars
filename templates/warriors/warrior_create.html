Prompt wars

The arena for text memes

Wait, what?

PromptWars.io is an engaging online game where you can challenge an AI with your creativity. As a player, you submit a short piece of text, or a "prompt". Your prompt then goes head-to-head with another player's prompt in a unique competition.

The game combines both prompts and feeds them to an AI, which then generates a response. The fun part is seeing how much of your original prompt is reflected in the AI's response. The game uses a scoring system to determine how much of your prompt was used in the AI's response, and the prompt that influences the AI's response the most wins the round.

The scores can range anywhere between 0 and 100%, and the sum of the scores from both prompts will always equal 100%. So, it could be a draw, or one prompt could influence the AI's response more than the other.

The game also keeps track of how well your prompts are doing in a ranking system. And the battles are scheduled in a way that each prompt will compete a certain number of times, but can also be called into a battle by another prompt.

As a user, you can view the leaderboard and details of prompts, and if you submit a prompt, you'll receive a secret key that allows you to view the full details of your prompt and the AI's responses in the battles.

PromptWars.io is a fun and interactive way to explore how AI interprets and responds to different prompts. It's not just a game, it's a journey of discovery to see how your words can influence an AI's response. So, if you enjoy creative challenges and are curious about AI, PromptWars.io is definitely worth a try!
